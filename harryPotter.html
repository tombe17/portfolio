Harry Potter Experience

The Project

Alongside a group nicknamed the Flock, we created a prototype experience that would be for Freshman at BYU. We looked at creating connections between students and trying to foster lasting connections. We interviewed several freshmen and learned about their needs to belong at BYU. We used Lucid to help facilitate our ideation process.



Focusing on our POV

We first had two activities that we seperated groups into. After looking at our feedback we learned that we were straying from our POV so we pivoted and focused on what would foster those connections and designed our experience on that.



The Resolution

We ended up with a very immersive experience that helped foster connections between participants and learned about the design thinking process. I would love to keep iterating and improving our design.
